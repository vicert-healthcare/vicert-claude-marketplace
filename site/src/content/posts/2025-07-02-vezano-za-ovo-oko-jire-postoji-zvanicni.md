Vezano za ovo oko JIRE - postoji i “zvanicni” Atlassianov MCP server koji moze da se integrise u Claude, cursor, etc…. Evo ga [ovde za Claude](https://community.atlassian.com/forums/Atlassian-Platform-articles/Using-the-Atlassian-Remote-MCP-Server-beta/ba-p/3005104) a evo ga [ovde za druge apps](https://community.atlassian.com/forums/Atlassian-Platform-articles/Atlassian-Remote-MCP-Server-beta-now-available-for-desktop/ba-p/3022084). Jedino sto ponekada ne radi - vraca Internal server error s’ vremena na vreme, ali valjda ce da ga poprave.
