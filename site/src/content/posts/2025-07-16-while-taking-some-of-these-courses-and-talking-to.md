While taking some of these courses and talking to people from Stanford that I met during a recent event, I realized that much of these programs were created by medical professionals/lecturers who are very fluent in both the clinical field and computer science. 

Therefore, many of these courses are primarely for providers, but can also be beneficial for others. 

So maybe one piece of advice I could give is to really explore the particular field and the target group to ensure that:

a) you know what you really want to accomplish with learning and certifying in the specific field

b) that the course is right for you in terms of the knowledge you want to get out of it
