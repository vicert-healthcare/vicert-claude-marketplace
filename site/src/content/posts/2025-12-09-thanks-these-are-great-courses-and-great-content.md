Thanks @Milos Djakovic These are great courses and great content - everyone is encouraged to read them, of course. However, at a current moment, our focus should remain on figuring out the **basics** and **elementary** usage of these AI tools and **applying them on our current projects**. We all need to become fluent in day-to-day usage of CC and similar tools beyond tab-completions-on-steroids and “fix this bug” level of prompting. We need to be done with basics first, and my assessment is that we are not there yet, except maybe few individuals.
