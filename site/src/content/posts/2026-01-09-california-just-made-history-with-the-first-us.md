🤖 **California Just Made History with the First U.S. Frontier AI Law**

Quick heads up: California's **Transparency in Frontier AI Act (SB 53)** was signed into law back in September 2025, **and as of January 1, 2026, it's now fully in effect.**

**What's the TL;DR?**
The biggest AI companies (think OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, etc.) now have to:
• Publish safety frameworks explaining how they prevent serious AI harms
• Report critical safety incidents to the state within 15 days
• Protect employees who blow the whistle on safety concerns
• Release transparency reports before launching new AI models
**Why should we care?**
This is the first U.S. law specifically targeting frontier AI safety — moving from voluntary self-regulation to actual legal requirements. Other states (like New York) are watching closely, and this could become a model for national policy.
Penalties: up to $1M per violation.
