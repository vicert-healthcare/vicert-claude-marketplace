Good PR, owning up the problem.

We humans tend to hold machines up to a much higher standard then we hold our selves to - a “zero-day” defect in our little EPROM.

It be interesting to do a study on “hallucinations”, errors and mistakes humans make, from story point estimates to commits we make to our friends and partners (uff!), all the way to decisions morons made, and will likely make yet again, to start World Wars. It may find that machines are doing OK already.
