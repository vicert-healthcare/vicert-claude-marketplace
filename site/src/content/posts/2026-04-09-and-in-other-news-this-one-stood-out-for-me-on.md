And in other news, this one stood out for me:

On **April 7, 2026**, Anthropic announced **Claude Mythos Preview**, a new model it says is unusually strong at cybersecurity work. In Anthropic’s own writeup, it says the model found **thousands of high-severity vulnerabilities**, including issues in **every major operating system and web browser** it tested, and that the oldest publicly discussed one was a **27-year-old bug in OpenBSD**. Anthropic also says the model could autonomously turn some vulnerabilities into working exploits.
