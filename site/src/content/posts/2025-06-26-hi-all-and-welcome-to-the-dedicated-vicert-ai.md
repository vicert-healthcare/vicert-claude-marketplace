Hi all, and welcome to the dedicated Vicert AI channel!

We will be using this channel to drive the **Vicert AI initiative** and as a main place for discussion around it.

To kick things off, I am sharing the recording of the latest [AI All hands session from 6/26](https://drive.google.com/file/d/16BtgMxoq2Kpfp-8c4AubsihzwKi-Sz4I/view?usp=drive_link). Rest of the recordings you can find [here](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1IISvnQr8wf8h0GNvA9hUQCgleXlfCJr2).

As we discussed today, a part of measuring the adoption and success of the initiative is looking at metrics. We have started reporting on tool usage, but will also be looking at contributions to the initiative - mainly individual activity in **this channel** and in the [Engineer Runbook](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1p7WIS5ih55h3_OoaQHjSrHcxXfrhCsEwOMbHMyZCHkI/edit?tab=t.0).
• The initial expectation is that **everyone** will post at least once in this channel by end of next week. This will help it gain traction and foster the culture of sharing. Your contribution can be anything AI related, really, but you should focus on things that will most benefit the group - so sharing experiences, good prompt examples, Cursor rules, etc.
• Going forward - it is expected that everyone makes **at least one meaningful contribution** to the Engineer Runbook per month.
• **Attendance** of the AI initiative All Hands calls is being measured from today - so if you can't make the call (at least remotely) then please let me know in advance!
• A separate Jira ticket [DL-30 AI Initiative](https://vicert.atlassian.net/browse/DL-30) has been created. This should make it easier for you to keep track of time that you've invested in the AI initiative. **Please use it to log time, every time you make a contribution.**
The next AI All Hands will be scheduled for the **week of 7/7.**  By then we will be able to discuss first report iterations and your contributions to this channel and Runbook doc. There will also be time reserved for strictly technical discussions, **so please come prepared**! (Topics will include MCP's, Cursor, AI tool usage legality - a more detailed agenda will be shared in advance)

Please **react to this message** so I know you’ve read and understood it.
Let’s keep the momentum, and this channel, going!

Thanks everyone! 😊 🚀 🤖 :vicert:
