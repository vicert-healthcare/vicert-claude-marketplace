`Prompts alone satisfy a scenario, but don't build the structure of the solution to accommodate future scenario`

Sentences that i deeply agree with and resonate with me a lot.
Which is something that needs a lot of attention when working with LLMs
From mu experi3mce no matter how you consreuct agent, skills or any other file, they tend to lean onto finishing the task at hand without caring for broader context (of course they even cant because of their contex window), thus reimplementing something that already exists.
So much focus and care is needed for refactoring part so that we do not become a victim of "tousands cut" problem.
