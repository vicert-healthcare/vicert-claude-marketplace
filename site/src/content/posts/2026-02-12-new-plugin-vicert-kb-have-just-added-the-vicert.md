**New Plugin: vicert-kb** 🚀

  I have just added the Vicert Knowledge Base plugin to our Claude Code marketplace. It gives Claude instant access to all 18 of our success
  stories — AI agents, EHR integration, cloud infrastructure, workflow automation, patient engagement, and more.

  Use it when writing proposals, pitch emails, marketing content, or anytime you need to reference our track record with concrete metrics and
  case studies.

  Install:
  `/plugin install vicert-kb@vicert-internal`

  Then just ask Claude things like `"find me a relevant case study for a hospital prospect interested in AI"` or invoke directly with

```  /vicert-kb:vicert-kb```
