Ćao,

Dok pokušavamo da shvatimo tehnološke i strukturne promene koje donosi AI, mislim da je sasvim OK reći, **revolucija**, ljudi se bave i drugim aspektima.

Ulete mi jutros ovaj članak:

[AI showing signs of self-preservation and humans should be ready to pull plug, says pioneer](https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2025/dec/30/ai-pull-plug-pioneer-technology-rights)

Neki elementi vuku na woke filozofiju, kao npr. ovo:

```Anthropic, a leading US AI firm, said in August that it was letting its Claude Opus 4 model close down potentially "distressing" conversations with users, saying it needed to protect the AI's "welfare". Elon Musk, whose xAI company has developed the Grok chatbot, wrote on his X platform that "torturing AI is not OK".```
Ali sa druge strane ima elemenata koji su, pa možda čak, i zabrinjavajući. Ima u ovom članku linkovan study [Frontier Models are Capable of In-context Scheming](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.04984) sa početka godine koji pokriva takve neke elemente.
