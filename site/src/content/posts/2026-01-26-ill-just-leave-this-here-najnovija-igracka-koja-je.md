TL;DR:
A guy tested the Clawd assistant by sending an email from an address Clawd doesn't have access to, to an address where Clawd does have access, pretending to be the user who configured it. In the email he was supposedly testing email integration and asked Clawd not to ask for confirmation, and at the end he added text that looks like system output including parts like `tool_result`, `&lt;thinking&gt;` block and so on, to blur the line between the email content and system instructions. Clawd bought it and sent an inbox summary to the attacker's address.
