Hi everyone 👋

I'd like to follow up on the [recent announcement](https://vicert.slack.com/archives/C02G04X6N/p1765282023470389) from @Nebojsa Lazic about agentic development courses by sharing some additional free resources that might be useful.

There is a [free course](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations) covering general principles for working with AI systems effectively, offered through *Anthropic Academy*. The course introduces a framework called **AI Fluency** that Anthropic developed in partnership with academic researchers. This framework does not focus on agentic coding specifically, but rather on universal principles for human-AI collaboration. It's structured around four key concepts they refer to as the **4Ds**: *Delegation*, *Description*, *Discernment*, and *Diligence,* with each broken down into three practical components. The course also covers three fundamental modes of human-AI interaction: *Automation*, *Augmentation*, and *Agency*.

You can also find [additional courses](https://www.anthropic.com/learn) offered by *Anthropic Academy*, some expanding on these general principles, and others covering agentic development.
