# Vicert Knowledge Base

This skill gives you access to Vicert's complete portfolio of 18 success stories spanning AI agent systems, EHR integration, cloud infrastructure, workflow automation, patient engagement, and more — all with live URLs you can cite.

## How to Use This Skill

### For Sales Materials & Proposals

When writing proposals, capability decks, or emails to prospects, match their needs to relevant stories. Lead with the business outcome (cost savings, time reduction, etc.), then briefly describe the solution, and link to the full story. Prospects in the same segment (hospital, payer, health-tech startup) respond best to stories from similar organizations.

### For Marketing Content

When writing blog posts, social media, website copy, or case study summaries, use the stories as source material. Pull specific metrics, describe the challenge-solution arc, and always link back to the story page. The three AI stories (voice agent, chart review, clinical notes) are Vicert's most forward-looking and differentiated content.

### For Internal Reference

When someone asks "have we done anything with FHIR?" or "what AI projects do we have?", use the catalog below to quickly find and cite relevant stories.

### Citation Format

Always provide the live URL when referencing a story. The URL pattern is:

```
https://vicert.ai/stories/{story-id}.html
```

The stories listing page is at: **https://vicert.ai/stories.html**
